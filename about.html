ABOUT / MANUAL

YELLOW BASKET - NETOPIA SUKI MARKET is an artist-built micro-website imagining the Pinoy early-internet retail landscape. It emulates Netopia terminals, CD-R King checkout counters, sari-sari POS, and the social life around them.

How to use

  1. ENTER the Netopia terminal (index).
  2. Add fake items to your cart.
  3. Fill Name & Address on the checkout screen and choose a fake payment mode (Gcrash, Crash Out, PayMoLater, etc.).
  4. Print / download receipt as .txt.
